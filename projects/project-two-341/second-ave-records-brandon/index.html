About Us

2nd Avenue Records was established in 1982 as an independent owned record store located in downtown Portland, Oregon. Originally only 600 square feet, situated in a quintessential historic brick and mortar storefront, the business has since expanded to even more comfortable quarters in the same building which is known as the Governor Building. Our stock consists of new and used records, cds and cassettes representing many genres, including rock (classic and current), more favorites also include our shelves that we keep

full of punk, metal, rap, 60s, 50s, soul & funk, jazz, blues, reggae, ska, surf, rockabilly and more. We endeavor to carry the best titles available to us and strive to constantly improve our stock so you can find what you’re looking for. In addition, we have a HUGE selection of music-related T-Shirts, including many classic rock, metal, punk, reggae and rap shirts. New shipments arrive quite frequently, providing a good selection of new shirts and restocks of old favorites. People who buy records here always come back.

Directions and Contact Info

2nd Avenue Records is located centrally in downtown Portland at 400 SW 2nd Avenue, which is at the intersection of SW 2nd and Stark Street. This is one block north of the west end of the Morrison Bridge, or it is just about four blocks south of

the west end of the Burnside Bridge. Our stock consists of new and used records, cds and cassettes representing many genres, including rock (classic & current), We make sure to carry a diverse collection of music for all listeners

For those traveling by light rail, the store is just one block west of the SW 1st and Oak stop. For out-of-town customers, please be advised that most light rail travel within the downtown area is free of charge.

Feel free to call the store if you are having difficulties. just remember that 2nd avenue records is located on the corner of SW 2nd and stark which is on a one way street.

Metered parking is generally available on the streets immediately adjacent to the store, and commercial parking lots also surround the store.
