Welcome to Maru

Yataimura is translated to English as “Food Cart Village.” In Japan at night, lighted food carts flood the town streets with appetizing snack foods. Yataimura Maru restaurant brings this exciting Japanese tradition to you.

As a restaurant that mirrors Japanese Yataimura, we serve cutting edge Japanese pub food with a blend of modern and traditional Japanese dishes. Our menu features handmade ramen noodles, sushi, yakimono (grilled skewers), and many other famous Japanese street foods. We serve the food that we hand-picked straight from traditional food carts, accompanied by the sake and beer that perfectly compliment our menu.
