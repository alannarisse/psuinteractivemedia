Disclaimer: While Akasaru Ramen provides gluten-free alternatives, cross contamination is still a possibility in the kitchen. About

Hi there!

Come hang out with us at Akasaru Ramen, where noodles are plentiful, and vibes are free. We use chicken broth for a lighter, cleaner take on ramen, and always source the freshest ingredients we can.
Grab a friend, get a bowl,
and celebrate the pure awesomeness
of ramen!

"I'm vegetarian / vegan / gluten-free!"

Awesome! At Akasaru Ramen, we're happy to accommodate your dietary needs. Dare we say- vegans and vegetarians love us! For customers with celiac, many of our ingredients are prepared vegan and gluten-free friendly, but cross contamination is still a possibility due to the size of our kitchen. While some with gluten-sensitivity may not consider this an issue, we strongly recommend taking this into consideration if you are celiac. Don't be afraid to ask questions when you're here. We're only here to help!
