PARTS

Hover and click each part to learn more!

START SWITCH

Starts the engines and gets the table ready to groove

RECORD

Records are made out of vinyl and spin on the platter.

RECORD LABEL

The label is a sticker artists put on their record to show the artist, band, etc.

STYLIS/NEEDLIE

The needle attaches to the cartridge which converts the movement of the needle into an electrical signal.

TONE-ARM

Holds the cartridge and allows the needle to ride in the grooves of the record.

CUEING DEVICE

Lifts and lowers the needle from the record. Can be automatic or manual.
