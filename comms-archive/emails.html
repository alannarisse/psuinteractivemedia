    There are two popular questions I'm getting, so here are the answers to those:

    Problem: Images work fine locally in my homework but they don't work on github.com

    Solution! You probably have one of two issues. The most popular issue is that you are using a mix of lowercase and uppercase folders and filenames. For instance, maybe you named your folder "Images" but in html you are using "images." this will work locally, but not on the server! Find out more here in this handy video! https://youtu.be/ftWedsSSC-s And the second most popular image problem is also later on in the video.

    Problem: I don't know where to submit my design journal entry for this week. Solution: If you have already posted a link to your homework 1, and you committed and pushed your design journal changes to github.com, you are done! I already have your design journal link.
