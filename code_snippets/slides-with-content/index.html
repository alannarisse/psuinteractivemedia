The Theater


The CineMagic is a single screen movie theater that originally opened in 1914 as the Palm, making it one of the oldest theaters on the west coast.

In the last 100 years the theater has taken many names, including the Hawthorne (1915), the Star Hawethorne (1934), the Zephyr (1937), the Plaza (1938), the Fine Arts (1957), and the Columbia (1987).

In 1991, current owner Chuck Nakvasil purchased the theater, and renovated it as the CineMagic.
