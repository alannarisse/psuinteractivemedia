Orcas

What Makes Them Special

Orcas, or killer whales, are the largest of the dolphins and one of the world's most powerful predators. They're immediately recognizable by their distinctive black-and-white coloring. Smart and social, orcas make a wide variety of sounds, and each pod has distinctive noises that its members will recognize even at a distance.
